## Ultrareading

2022-11-04 19:20

Ultrareading is a way of reading multiple books in one day just by doing the following

1) Speed Read
2) Subvocalize
3) Practical Books Only
4) Read Table of Contents
5) Read Intros
6) Read Conclusions
7) Journal
