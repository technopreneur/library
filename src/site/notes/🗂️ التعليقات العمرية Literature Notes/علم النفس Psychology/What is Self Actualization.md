## What is Self Actualization

2022-09-24 11:05

Drawing from the Maslow Hierarchy of Needs the last and final level called “Self Actualization” can be defined but not limited to as 5 qualities

Self Actualization: 
1) Independence
2) Awareness
3) Creativity
4) Originality
5) Courage
